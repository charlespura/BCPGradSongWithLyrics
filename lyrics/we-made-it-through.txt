We Made It Through
Graduation Tribute Song 2026 (For Teachers & Parents)

(Verse 1) 
The days were long, the nights were tough, 
We dreamed, we prayed, and that was enough.
Through every test, through every fight, 
We found our way, we reached the light. 

(Pre-Chorus) 
And now we stand with heads held high, 
With grateful hearts we testify. 
For every step, we know it’s true, 
Our teachers and parents, we thank you. 

(Chorus) 
We made it through, together we shine, 
Our dreams are here, the future’s mine. 
With love and faith, we’ll see it through, This victory belongs to you. 
(Repeat last line with power: This victory belongs to you!) 

(Verse 2) 
Our teachers showed us the guiding way, Their wisdom bright as the break of day. Our parents’ love, through thick and thin, The reason why we rise, we win. 

(Pre-Chorus) 
So here we stand, a brand new start, 
With courage burning in our hearts. 
The journey’s done, the dream’s in view, Today we celebrate with you. 




(Chorus) 
We made it through, together we shine,
 Our dreams are here, the future’s mine. With love and faith, we’ll see it through, 
This victory belongs to you. 

(Bridge) 
For every sleepless night we cried, 
For every moment we survived, 
We lift our hands, our thanks we bring, 
This is the song of hope we sing. 

(Final Chorus) 
We made it through, together we shine,
 Our dreams are here, the future’s mine. 
With love and faith, we’ll see it through, 
This victory belongs to you! 

(Outro) 
Today’s the day, our dreams come true, 
And all our thanks belong to you.
